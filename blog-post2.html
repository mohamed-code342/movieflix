Oscars 2025: Big Wins and Historic Moments

The 2025 Oscars delivered unforgettable moments, from surprise wins to emotional speeches. Christopher Nolan finally took home Best Director for “Oppenheimer”, while Emma Stone stunned audiences by winning Best Actress for her role in “The Unknown Light”.

The ceremony was held at the Dolby Theatre in Hollywood and was hosted by Maya Rudolph. The night also saw a strong presence of international cinema, with Japan’s “Silent Echoes” winning Best International Feature.

Highlights included a moving tribute to late director Richard Linklater, a special live performance by Billie Eilish, and a standing ovation for Meryl Streep as she presented the Lifetime Achievement Award.

From glamorous red carpet looks to groundbreaking wins, the Oscars 2025 proved to be one of the most memorable editions in recent history.
